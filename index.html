About Me

My name is Runyuan Chen, and I go by Jason. I am a thriving UX practitioner known for my highly collaborative and versatile aptitude to work through the entire product cycle - from researching and defining user needs to designing and validating products. I have almost 2 years experiences designing for various environments including startup, agency, institute, and museum. I have recently delivered a high quality UX website roadmap to the Burke Museum.

Outside of my work, I love to explore different concepts via side projects. I have a particular passion to design for inspiring personal growth, and many of my side projects have been recognized in renowned conferences such as CHI Conference.

I am available for freelance as well as full-time/internship opportunities in the UX industry. I would love to hear from you!
