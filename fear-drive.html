Fear Drive

My design stories at a natural history museum

STORY

In May 2017, I attended CHI Conference 2017 and learned how experimental design could push the boundaries of science and engineering.

In August 2017, I found myself thinking about the multifaceted nature as a person through reading my journal. I found that there was something that I fear, but somehow that thing has been motivating me to achieve more.

I want to learn more about this "thing" and I was intrigued by experimental design.

So, I said to myself, why don't I investigate this "thing" by doing an experimental design project on it?

PROJECT INSTALLATION HAPPENING!

After 4 months of conceptions, ideation, and implementation, this project will be on public display at my very own department building tomorrow, Dec.14th! Details of the story and final product will be uploaded after the installation. You could view a bit of my process and sneak peek below.
